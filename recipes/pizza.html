Pizza

Description

This time, I will show you the recipe for pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 °F/45 °C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 °F/230 °C. Oil a pizza pan lightly.
  2. Prepare all ingredients.
  3. Pour warm water into a bowl and add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy for around 10 minutes.
  4. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture and beat it until smooth. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Move dough onto a lightly floured surface and form into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Put it on the pizza pan.
  7. Spread sauce and preffered toppings over the crust.
  8. Bake in the oven until golden brown for 15–20 minutes. Remove and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
